A 3-Sentence Structure

“I’ve missed feeling close to you lately.
Could we take some time just for us tonight?
If it’s not the right moment, tell me what would feel good instead.”

Appreciation. Request. Choice.

This keeps consent inside the moment. It lowers pressure. It keeps dignity intact.
